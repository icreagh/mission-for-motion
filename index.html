Our Mission

At Mission For Motion, we believe that mobility is a fundamental human right, not a luxury. Our purpose is to eliminate the barrier of cost for individuals suffering from debilitating joint conditions in underserved regions by providing world-class, no-cost total hip and knee replacements.

At Mission For Motion, we provide world-class, no-cost total hip and knee joint replacements for individuals suffering from debilitating joint conditions in underserved regions. We handle everything from patient selection and pre-operative care to the surgery itself and post-operative rehabilitation, ensuring a comprehensive path to renewed mobility.

Our success is built on a global partnership of compassionate professionals. We collaborate with orthopedic surgeons, anesthesiologists, physical therapists, and nurses who volunteer their time and expertise. We also partner with hospitals that donate their facilities and local community leaders who help us identify patients most in need of our care.

We operate through a model of targeted medical missions funded by generous donors and corporate partners. Each mission is meticulously planned to maximize impact, from sourcing donated surgical implants to coordinating logistics for our volunteer teams. This efficient, focused approach allows us to transform donations directly into life-changing procedures.
